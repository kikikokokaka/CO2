What is the CO2aid?

The CO2 aid is an applications, which helps you to get an inside on your CO2 footprint and why it is higher or lower in comparison to others. It gives you inside on how your behaviour impacts your CO2 footprint and also provides you with practical solutions to reduce your impact on the environment. Your successes are shown to other participants, so they can learn and this way reduce their own footprint. By working together the CO2 aid aims to reduce everybodyâ€™s CO2 footprint to their theoretical minimum. You can thus be proud and satisfied when you have achieved this goal and you know you have done your very best!

What we want to achieve with you:

  • You get an inside of your own CO2 footprint
  • You learn what behaviour influences your CO2 footprinte
  • You can see what other people do to reduce their impact
  • You can see that you can actually easily and enjoyably reduce your CO2 footprint
  • You see how big your impact can be when working together with other people!

So, how does it work?

First of all, you have to make an account. Just so you know you can make the account anonymous, by using a nice nick name! After you have created an account you will have access to your private page, where you can provide the app with information concerning your CO2 influencing behaviour! No worries, you only have to answer some questions, so you are done in no time!(this behaviour can ofcourse be update, cause you will of course improve and the app will therefore also ask for a weekly update!)

After you have provided the information the app calculates your current CO2 footprint and places you somewhere in the ranking.There you can compare your CO2 footprint with other people, who live in the Netherlands. Some general information concerning your living conditions and CO2 influencing behaviour is also posted there, to help you find people in similar living conditions as you are.

Than on the messenger page information can be exchanged and advise can be given!

Than we have the company page. The company page provides the oppertunity to quickly find a sustainable company that might be able to help you with a problem concening sustainability. On the company page you can read a small summary of what the company does. Furthermore you can read reviews of costumors and the rankings they give to the company. So to sum it up:

  • You make an account, which can either be anonymous or shows who you are
  • You provide the app with information about your behaviour that influences your CO2 footprint (you just have to answer some easy questions)
  • You are placed in a ranking where you can compare your out put and some behavioural asplects with other people
  • On the messangers page you can either give or receive information, which is aimed to reduce your CO2 footprint in a practical and doable way
  • You can on a weekly basis update your CO2 influencing behaviour and see what the influence is on your CO2 footprint!
  • You can get a good overview of sustainable companies, from which you can get advise and you can see what other people's oppinion is of those companies.
